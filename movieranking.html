The Best Movies According to Manas

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

1. Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

This is one of the best out there, motivates one to live their life to the fullest as you only get in once.

2. Lakshya

This movie revolves around a young lad Karan Shergill, who goes on to become an officer in the Indian Army.

3. Dangal

Good movie, tells the bond between a father and his daughters beautifully.
